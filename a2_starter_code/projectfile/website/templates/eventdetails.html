Mid-Autumn Festival 2025

Event Status

The status of Mid-Autumn Festival 2025 is: Open

Event Category

The category of Mid-Autumn Festival 2025 is: Cultural

Description

The Mid-Autumn Festival is a Chinese cultural food festival surrounding Mooncakes. Visit now at the Brisbane venue as many gather to celebrate Chinese food culture.

Date

The Mid-Autumn Festival 2025 will be held on 06/10/2025, with start and end times indicated below.

Start Time

The Mid-Autumn Festival 2025 will begin at 4:00pm AEST UTC/GMT +10. The Mid-Autumn Festival is known for having a large line of eager participants, so it is recommended to arrive early.

End Time

The Mid-Autumn Festival 2025 will conclude at 11:00pm AEST UTC/GMT +10. The Mid-Autumn Festival ends late at night. It is recommended to prepare transport for your departure prior to the event.

Venue

The Mid-Autumn Festival 2025 is located at Chinatown Mall. 33 Duncan St, Fortitude Valley QLD 4006.

Vendors

The following Vendors will be participating in the Mid-Autumn Festival 2025: Sunnybank Jade Moon Bakery, Story Bridge Lotus & Yolk, Riverlight Mooncake House, Fortitude Valley Osmanthus Patisserie, South Bank Black Sesame Co.

Sampling

The Mid-Autumn Festival 2025 does not provide Free Sampling. Vendors pre-prepare Mooncakes rather than preparing for order, meaning stock is often limited at this festival. We apologise for this inconvenience.

Takeaway

The Mid-Autumn Festival 2025 provides takeaway options. Although Mooncakes are commonly consumed at stalls served with traditional green tea, they are also perfect for storing in takeaway containers to consume at a later time.

Tickets

Amount of tickets remaining: 200 Available

Choose from child or adult tickets to participate.
